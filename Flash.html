Flash

The Flash made his first appearance in Flash Comics #1 released in 1940. Jay Garrick, gained the power of the "speed force" as DC's first "Flash" with several more to come over the years, including the current well known Flash Barry Allen. The Flash is crucial to DC's story structure as he introduced the Multiverse to the DC empire. The Flash's skill sets include, but are not limited to, the ability to think and move at light speed as well as having god-like endurance.
