Spawn

Spawn is an antihero appearing in Image comics. Albert was enlisted in the Marine Corps and while on a mission he was killed and sent to hell. While in hell Albert made a deal with the devil and became a hellspawn. Hellspawns are warriors for the devil's army that recieve superhuman and demonic abilities. Spawn decides to use his powers for good instead of evil which results in other demons attempting to bring him back to hell multiple times.
