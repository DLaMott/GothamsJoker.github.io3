Joker

The Joker is one of DC's most iconic villains of all time. The Joker is batman's longtime foe and the two have been in a war for Gotham since the beginning of Batman's arrival in Gotham. Joker's main plot is to continue having the Batman attempt to capture him and or break Batman's rule of no killing by tempting him to kill The Joker. The joker is an agent of chaos with no moral boundaries. The Joker's name and backstory is unknown but multiple comic writers have given their version on the Joker over time. The most notable and widely accepted is the story of The Killing Joke.
