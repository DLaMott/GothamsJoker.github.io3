Marv

Marv is the main character in the DarkHorse series Sin City. Marv seeks to claim revenge on the killers of the woman whom he loved the most. Marv utilizes weapons and torture methods to fight and overcome his enemies. He suffers from a mental condition that causes him to randomly become confused at times. Marv is a large built character and made multiple appearances in the DarkHorse graphic novels.
